Reading for lab classes

Chapter 8 of Ladefoged and Johnson (2015) is the recommended intro to acoustic phonetics and is available to online via the library. For more in-depth reading try Johnson (2012) and Ladefoged (1996). For a Sociophonetics slant try Thomas (2011).
